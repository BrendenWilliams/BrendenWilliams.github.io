My BiographySeptember 30, 2016

I'm currently the head of the Computer Science department at Brescia University in Owensboro, Kentucky. I received my PhD in Computer Science, more specifically Artificial Intelligence, at the University of Southern Queensland in Toowoomba, Australia. I received my Bachelor of Science and Master of Science degrees in Computer Science at the University of West Indies at Mona, Jamaica. I love Artificial Intelligence and its application in Medicine and Security hence my PhD is based on Medical Signal Processing focusing on Signals such as EEGs. I also enjoy teaching and making a difference in students lives. Can always find some of my publications on www.academia.edu, or you can see them under the Publications tab. Below you can see a map with the locations of where I received my PhD and Masters degrees. The bolder X mark, in the Carribean, symbols where I received my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science. The lighter X in Australia shows where I received my PhD in Computer Science, specifically in Artificial Intelligence.
